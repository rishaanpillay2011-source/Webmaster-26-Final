About Pathfinders

Northern Virginia is a diverse and vibrant region, but it is also one where the cost of living can create significant challenges for many families. Pathfinders was built with a singular focus: to bridge the gap between those in need and the incredible organizations working tirelessly on the frontlines of our community.

Whether it's a family seeking food security in Fairfax, an individual looking for affordable housing in Arlington, or a student needing educational support in Loudoun, we believe that access to essential services should be intuitive, dignified, and barrier-free. Below are some of the cornerstone organizations that make this mission possible.

Cornerstone Partners

Food for Others

Food Security

As one of the largest safety-net organizations in Northern Virginia, Food for Others distributes food to over 1,800 families every week through emergency neighborhood sites and local partnerships across Fairfax County.

Learn More →

Northern Virginia Family Service

Comprehensive Care

NVFS provides wraparound services addressing the root causes of poverty. Serving over 40,000 individuals annually, they offer emergency shelter, early childhood education, health access, and workforce development.

Learn More →

Cornerstones

Housing & Support

Operating in the Reston/Herndon corridor, Cornerstones runs the Embry Rucker Community Shelter, a robust food pantry, and affordable housing communities to help individuals rebuild their lives with dignity.

Learn More →

Legal Aid Justice Center

Legal Advocacy

LAJC partners with communities to fight systemic injustice by providing free civil legal aid, protecting low-income Virginians from unlawful evictions, and advocating for immigrant rights and equitable access.

Learn More →

United Community

Community Empowerment

Focused on the Route 1 corridor in Alexandria, United Community empowers families with a multi-generational approach that includes basic needs assistance, ESL classes, and neighborhood community centers.

Learn More →

FACETS

Homelessness Prevention

FACETS opens doors by helping parents, their children, and individuals who suffer the effects of poverty in Fairfax County. Their programs include emergency shelter, permanent supportive housing, and a free community resource center.

Learn More →
